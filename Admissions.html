Departments

VIT-AP offers distinctive education through its Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Ph.D. Programmes. The teaching-learning process at the institute prepares students for the future. They are involved in an active process of applied learning with the help of experienced faculty who enable the students to channel their talent and intellect to contribute towards the development and sustainability of society.

With an emphasis on innovation and applying technology to improve life, the institute offers the following programmes for students at various levels:

  • B.Tech.
  • BBA
  • LAW
  • B.A. - M.A.
  • B.Sc.
  • M.Sc.
  • B.Com.
  • M.Tech.
  • International
  • Ph.D.
